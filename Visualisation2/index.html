VEHICLE ACCIDENTS IN VICTORIA (2017-2019)

INTRODUCTION

Victoria, Australia witnessed a critical period in road safety from 2017 to 2019, with a multitude of vehicle accidents impacting lives and communities. This visualization delves into the rich data collected during this time, shedding light on the geographical distribution, common contributing factors, and trends in accidents across this three-year span. By exploring this comprehensive dataset, we aim to not only raise awareness about the challenges posed by road safety but also provide valuable insights for stakeholders and policymakers to improve road infrastructure, enforce safety measures, and ultimately, reduce accidents and their associated human and economic costs.
